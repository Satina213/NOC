Random Walkers

First up we've got the tried and true random walker with one small upgrade. This little guy will walk randomly left or right and up or down each frame of animation.

This walker here uses Gaussian distribution to determine how much to move with a mean of 0 on each axis and a standard deviation of 1. So basically it can move up 3 spaces but it's rare, it sometimes moves 2 spaces but not really, and it most often moves 1 space.

This one is my favorite so far utilizing Perlin Noise. So each frame the direction it moves *is* random but it's going to be similiar to the direction it was already moving.

This is just another demonstration of Perlin Noise
